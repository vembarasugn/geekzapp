Air Pollution Awareness Art Contest

Go Green. Breathe Clean.

Air pollution is the fifth largest killer in India and fourth largest globally, kills more than 5.5 million people a year.

You could go days without food and hours without water, but you would last only a few minutes without air. On an average, each of us breathe over 3,000 gallons of air per day. Air is essential to live. However, we don't care about it, we pollute it by damaging trees, crops, other plants, lakes, animals and our own health. In addition, to damaging the natural environment, air pollution also damages buildings, monuments and statues.

There are actions each and every one of us can take to reduce air pollution such as carpooling, cycling and walking, to reduce carbon emissions and planting trees to increase oxygen.  Making positive changes will help prevent air pollution and help us breathe easier now and in the future!

Would you like to join us in creating awareness? Have your children and students draw an art which depicts how people can help protect their health, the air we breathe, and prevent air pollution. They could win exciting prizes while invited for the Geekz Art Gala on July 9th, 2017.
